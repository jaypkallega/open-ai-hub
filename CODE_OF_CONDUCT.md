# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, experience level,
gender, nationality, or background.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes:
- Being respectful and constructive in all feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behaviour includes:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Submitting deliberately harmful, misleading, or low-quality prompts
- Trolling or consistently unconstructive commentary

## Enforcement
Violations can be reported by:
- Opening a private GitHub Issue
- Using GitHub's private vulnerability reporting feature

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject any contribution
that does not align with this Code of Conduct.
